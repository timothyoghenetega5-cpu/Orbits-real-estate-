HOW WE WORK

A survey process built on accuracy, at every step.

Every plot we survey and every deal we broker follows the same disciplined method — so you can trust the boundary lines and the paperwork alike.

I.

Site Reconnaissance

We assess terrain, access and existing beacons before a single coordinate is logged.

II.

GPS & Instrument Survey

Total station and RTK-GPS equipment capture boundary data to within centimetres.

III.

Plan & Registration

Survey plans are drafted, cross-checked and lodged with the Oyo State Surveyor-General.

IV.

Handover & Title Support

You receive your beacon certificate and full guidance through C of O processing.
